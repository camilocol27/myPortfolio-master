Hi, my name is Cristian C. Nino


I am from Bogota, Colombia. I was raised in Queens, New York, from 2005 to 2009 I attended Queensbourough Community College and obtained an Associates degree in Digital Art and Design. I have thorough knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Quark-Xpress, Corel-Draw, Front-page and Dreamweaver.

In 2008, I started to work in Graphitech Printing Corp, in Queens NY. In this company I worked in the area of production, as pre-press, and graphic designer assistant. My daily duties were to create / update designs for all types of customers inclusive of restaurants, magazines, newspapers etc. In 2015, I have started to develop my knowledge to become a front end website builder. Through my studies at New York Code Design Academy (NYCDA), I learned web design using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Jquery, Boostrap, Sass, ajax, Json.js, GitHUb.
